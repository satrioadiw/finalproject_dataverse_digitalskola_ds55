.final project objective:
Building a predictive model capable of classifying individual income based on demographic and socioeconomic attributes in the dataset. Data source taken from Kaggle.com

Analysis of socioeconomic variables such as age, marital status, type of employment, number of hours worked per week, etc. is expected to provide insights into key factors that influence individual income levels.

The results of this analysis can be utilized in various fields, such as social policy planning, labor market analysis, and customer segmentation.
